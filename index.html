About the Conference

The department of Information technology of ITM is organizing the National Conference on Communication, Computational Intelligence and Learning (NCCCIL-21). This National Conference is a collaborative platform for academic experts, researchers and corporate professionals to enrich their knowledge in the automation and analysis of industry and business processes in a smart way. The two day conference includes invited talks and paper presentations focusing on the applications of Computational intelligence, Communication, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

  • Selected papers will be published by CRC Press, Taylor and Francis as a conference proceedings with additional charges and will be submitted to SCOPUS and Google Scholar for indexing.
  • Selected papers will be published in International Journal of Next-Generation Computing (IJNGC) Web of Science E-SCI journal
  • Selected papers will be published in IETE National Journal of Innovation & Research
    • E-certificate will be awarded to all registered participants of the conference.

Call for Papers

Papers are invited on (but not limited to) following topics:

  • Machine Learning
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Business intelligence and Management
  • Cloud & Grid Computing
  • Computational Science
  • Data Analytics and Data Mining
  • Data Science
  • Mobile Technologies
  • Nano Technology
  • Pattern Recognition
  • Privacy and Data Protection
  • Virtual and Augmented Reality
  • Image Processing and Computer Vision
  • Communication and Networking

All submitted papers will be blind peer reviewed by the competent reviewers. Your manuscript must be formatted with the template provided. You can download the paper template from here. Also CRC Press Permission Form and Consent to Publish Form should be completely filled and signed by all participants after accpetance of their research papers.
